About Me

>

Adrian Tran
Aspiring Engineer

I am an aspiring computer engineer with a strong background in UI/UX design, web development, communication, and game design.

I have spent 200+ hours spearheading the development and design decisions of a mobile racing game at the 2Axion Games offices in Cerritos, CA. While there, I collaborated with the in-house artist and devlopment team to further both the game and other projects.

In addition, I have worked under the tutelage of seasoned software and hardware engineers for an additional 150+ hours at the Milton Security Group in Fullerton, CA. By providing coding support in PHP, Swift, HTML, and CSS, I was able to assist with both front-end and back-end modifications to the company website and social medias.

Besides that, I was also actively involved in executive leadership positions for both the local and state-wide Japanese student association at UC Riverside. In my spare time, I enjoy exercising, writing, gaming, the guitar, and petting chubby cats.

Currently, I am a Senior at the University of California, Riverside, majoring in Computer Engineering.
